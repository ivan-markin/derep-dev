Miami Gold Resort

Lennar Corporation

The Miami Gold Resort is being built in West Miami. The façades were designed by the Dutch bureau de Architekten Cie, and the landscaping by the British bureau Gillespies. Here you can choose apartments with high ceilings, corner windows, and balconies with panoramic glazing.

About the developer

Lennar Corporation was founded in 2005. The main activity of the company is the construction of apartment buildings, including under the equity construction, and commercial real estate. It is a member of the self-regulatory organization "Builders' Alliance of America". The company is accredited with all banks in the U.S. that provide mortgages to the public, including those under the federal and regional programs "Housing for young families. For the 15 years of its activities in the construction market, the company has built and put into operation more than 100,000 square meters of the economy to premium class.
